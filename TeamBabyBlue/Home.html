The N-men are subspecies of humans who were born with extraordinary abilities. However due to their high functional skills they have trouble integrating in modern society. Preferring the company of the night and the light of the computer monitor. The N-men Institute For The Gifted was founded by Professor N(akov) in an attempt to guide, nurture and protect these extraordinary individuals. They fight for peace and equality between normal humans and software developers in a world where anti-programmer bigotry is fierce and widespread.
