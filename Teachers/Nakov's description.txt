Svetlin Nakov (also known as Professor N ) has 20 years experience of technical background as discoverer of people with superhuman powers. He founded X-Uni to teach mutants to coding and control their powers. Its first group of students are Ache Magneto, The Bankin Wolverine, Bogy The Cyclops and Rossy Storm. X-Uni's students consider him a visionary and often refer to their mission as "Nakov's dream".
